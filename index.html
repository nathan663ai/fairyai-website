Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fairy Dust and how does it work?

Fairy Dust is our creative currency:
• Stories & story continuations: 1 Fairy Dust each
• Characters, songs, & narrations: 2 Fairy Dust each
• You get 6 free to start
• Monthly subscriptions give you a dust top-up

Do unused Fairy Dust credits roll over?

Monthly Fairy Dust from a subscription plan resets each month. This allows us to offer the best possible value. Any Fairy Dust you purchase in separate bundles never expires!

Is there a free trial?

Absolutely! Every new account gets 6 free Fairy Dust credits to try everything the app has to offer—no credit card required. That's enough for 6 full creations (stories, characters, or songs).

Can multiple children use one account?

Yes! You can create unlimited character profiles on a single account, so each child can have their own stories and heroes. All content is saved to the one main account.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Of course. You can easily cancel your subscription at any time through the App Store or Google Play Store settings. You'll retain access for the remainder of your billing period.

Is my child's data safe?

Absolutely. FairyAI is COPPA compliant. We don't track your children, show ads, or sell data to anyone. Your stories and characters are private to your account. We use AI providers (OpenAI, Replicate) only to generate content—they don't store your prompts. Read our full Privacy Policy.

How long does story generation take?

Most stories generate in under 60 seconds. Characters with 6 art styles take 2-3 minutes. Songs take 3-5 minutes. You'll get a notification when they're ready, so you can do other things while waiting.

Do I need internet to use FairyAI?

You need internet to generate new content, but all your saved stories, characters, and songs are available offline once created. Perfect for car journeys and flights!

What ages is FairyAI suitable for?

FairyAI creates age-appropriate content for ages 2-12+. You can set the age range when creating stories, and our AI automatically adjusts vocabulary, themes, and complexity. Classic fairy tales in the Fairy Corner suit all ages.

Can I use FairyAI in other languages?

Yes! FairyAI supports 9 languages: English (US, UK, Australia), Spanish (Spain, Mexico), French, German, Italian, and Portuguese. You can create stories in any language and switch between them anytime.
