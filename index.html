FAQs

Quick answers to common questions.

How does Fairy Dust work?
Each generation uses a small amount of dust (story 1, character 2, song 2, narration 2). You start with 6 free dust. Subscriptions top you up monthly; one-off dust purchases never expire.
Is the content safe for children?
Yes. We combine age settings with layered filters, calm pacing and parental review tools. No ads. No selling data. GDPR/COPPA aligned.
What ages is FairyAI for?
Designed for ages 2–12+. The language, length and complexity adapt to your chosen age band.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Manage your plan via the App Store or Google Play. Your creations remain in your library.
