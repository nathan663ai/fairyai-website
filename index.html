Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fairy Dust and how does it work?

Fairy Dust is our simple in-app credit system. 1 Fairy Dust = 1 creation. You can create a story, a character, or a song all for the same price. You get 6 free to start, and our plans give you a monthly top-up.

Do unused Fairy Dust credits roll over?

Monthly Fairy Dust from a subscription plan resets each month. This allows us to offer the best possible value. Any Fairy Dust you purchase in separate bundles never expires!

Is there a free trial?

Absolutely! Every new account gets 6 free Fairy Dust credits to try everything the app has to offer‚Äîno credit card required. That's enough for 6 full creations (stories, characters, or songs).

Can multiple children use one account?

Yes! You can create unlimited character profiles on a single account, so each child can have their own stories and heroes. All content is saved to the one main account.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Of course. You can easily cancel your subscription at any time through the App Store or Google Play Store settings. You'll retain access for the remainder of your billing period.
