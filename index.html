Frequently Asked Questions

How does Fairy Dust work?
Fairy Dust is our virtual currency. Stories cost 1 dust, characters and songs cost 2 dust each. You get 6 free dust to start, then choose a monthly subscription that fits your needs. Unused monthly dust resets each month, but any dust you purchase separately never expires!
Is content safe for children?
Absolutely! Every story is filtered through advanced AI safety guidelines and automatically adapted to your selected age group. We're GDPR and COPPA compliant, with no ads and no data sharing. Parents have full control with parental dashboards and content review options.
What age groups is this for?
FairyAI is designed for children ages 2-12+. Stories automatically adapt to five age groups: 2-3 (simple words, 200 words), 4-6 (engaging plots, 550 words), 7-9 (complex adventures, 800 words), 10-12 (rich narratives, 1100 words), and 12+ (advanced stories). The Fairy Corner has content for all ages!
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! Cancel anytime directly through the App Store or Google Play Store. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No hassle, no questions asked. Plus, you can always come back - your characters and stories are saved!
How fast are stories created?
Stories are generated in about 60 seconds! Characters take about 90 seconds (6 different art styles to choose from), and songs are ready in about 2 minutes. Premium subscribers get priority generation for even faster results.
Do you store my child's data?
We only store what's necessary to provide the service (stories and characters you create). Photos are converted to cartoon characters and the original is deleted. We never collect personal information beyond what's required. You can export or delete all your data anytime. We're fully GDPR and COPPA compliant.
