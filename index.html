Frequently Asked Questions

How does Fairy Dust work?
Fairy Dust is our virtual currency. Stories cost 1 dust, characters and songs cost 2 dust each. You get 6 free dust to start, then choose a monthly subscription. Unused monthly dust resets each month, but purchased dust never expires!
Is content safe for children?
Absolutely! Every story is filtered through AI safety guidelines and adapted to your selected age group. We're GDPR and COPPA compliant, with no ads and no data sharing. Parents have full control.
What age groups is this for?
FairyAI is for children ages 2-12+. Stories automatically adapt to five age groups with appropriate vocabulary and complexity. The Fairy Corner has content for all ages!
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! Cancel anytime through the App Store or Google Play Store. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No hassle, no questions asked.
How fast are stories created?
Stories are generated in about 60 seconds! Characters take about 90 seconds (6 art styles to choose from), and songs are ready in about 2 minutes. Premium subscribers get priority generation.
