Create Your First Character

This is where the magic starts. You can create a character in three ways:

• Describe them with words ("A brave little hedgehog with a red cape")
• Upload a photo (your child, their pet, their favourite toy)
• Let your child draw them (snap a photo of their drawing)

Choose from 6 beautiful art styles, and watch as AI transforms your idea into a magical storybook character in seconds.

3

Generate a Story

Now for the fun part. You have two options:

Quick Story: Just say what you want in plain English. "A story about Leo the hedgehog learning to swim" or "An adventure where Bella finds a magic paintbrush."

Custom Story: Choose characters, setting, themes, and story length. Perfect control for specific bedtime needs.

Hit "Create" and in 30-60 seconds, you'll have a complete, beautifully illustrated story ready to read.

Read Together & Continue Tomorrow

Your story is ready! Read it together on screen, or tap the narration button to hear it read aloud in a professional voice.

Love the story? Save it to your library. Want to know what happens next? Tap "Continue Story" tomorrow night and the adventure continues right where you left off.
