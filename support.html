Support & Help

We're here to help. Most questions are answered below, but feel free to reach out if you need anything.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID (iOS) or Google Play account (Android), not through the FairyAI app directly.

For iPhone/iPad: Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → FairyAI → Cancel Subscription

For Android: Open Google Play Store → Profile Icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → FairyAI → Cancel Subscription

If you cancel, you'll still have access until the end of your current billing period.

Is my child's data safe?

Absolutely. FairyAI is COPPA compliant. We don't track your children, show ads, or sell data to anyone. The app is designed for parents to use—you create the stories, and your child enjoys them. We only collect data about your account usage (what features you use, when you generate content), not about your child. Any characters or stories you create are stored securely and are only visible to you.

How long does story generation take?

Most stories generate in under 60 seconds. Characters with 6 art styles also take less than a minute, even though we're creating multiple images for you. Songs typically take 90-120 seconds. We'll notify you when your content is ready if you leave the app.

Do I need internet to use FairyAI?

You need internet to generate new content, but all your saved stories, characters, and songs are available offline once created. Perfect for car journeys or flights!

What ages is FairyAI suitable for?

FairyAI creates age-appropriate content for ages 2-12+. When you set up your account, you select your child's age group, and our AI adjusts vocabulary, themes, and complexity accordingly. You can also customize content preferences in Settings.

Can I use FairyAI in other languages?

Yes! FairyAI supports 9 languages: English (US, UK, Australia), Spanish (Spain, Mexico), French, German, Italian, and Portuguese. You can set your preferred language in Settings, and all generated content will be in that language.

How does Fairy Dust work?

Fairy Dust is our credit system. Different features cost different amounts:

• 1 Fairy Dust: New story or story continuation
• 2 Fairy Dust: Custom character (with 6 art styles), song, or narration

You get 6 free Fairy Dust when you sign up to try everything. After that, you can purchase more credits or subscribe for monthly Fairy Dust.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account in the app. Your account will be immediately deactivated and scheduled for permanent deletion after 30 days. During those 30 days, you can email us at legal@fairyai.co.uk to recover your account if you change your mind. After 30 days, all your data (stories, characters, songs) will be permanently deleted.

You can also visit our Delete Account page for more information.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try these steps first:

1. Make sure you're running the latest version of FairyAI (check the App Store/Google Play)
2. Restart the app completely (force close and reopen)
3. Check your internet connection
4. If generating content fails, try again in a few minutes

If the problem persists, email us at support@fairyai.co.uk with your device type, app version, and what you were trying to do when the issue occurred.

Can I share stories I create with FairyAI?

Yes! You own all the content you generate using FairyAI. You can download stories, characters, and songs to your device and share them however you like—print them, share via messaging apps, post on social media, or even use them commercially if you want. We encourage parents to share their favorite stories with friends and family!
