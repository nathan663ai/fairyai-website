Why Use Fairy Corner Instead of Creating Stories?

Both are valuable—it depends on the moment.

✨ Use Custom Stories When...

  • ✓ You have time and energy
  • ✓ Your child wants to see their character in a story
  • ✓ You want to teach a specific lesson or moral
  • ✓ You're building an ongoing adventure series
  • ✓ You want something unique and personalized

📚 Use Fairy Corner When...

  • ✓ It's 8 PM and you're exhausted
  • ✓ You want a classic tale your child knows
  • ✓ You don't want to wait 30-90 seconds
  • ✓ You're low on Fairy Dust and saving it
  • ✓ You want predictable, trusted content

💡 Pro Tip for Parents

On nights when you're too tired to be creative, the Fairy Corner is your lifesaver. On nights when you have energy, create custom stories with your child's characters. Both are included with your subscription—use whichever fits the moment.
